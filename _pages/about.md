Hi there! I am an undergraduate from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence. Besides, I am a member of [Guozhi class](http://www.qingyuan.sjtu.edu.cn/c/Introductiongzb) (held by [Prof. Xiaoou Tang](https://www.ie.cuhk.edu.hk/faculty/tang-xiaoou-sean/)). Currently, I am a research intern at both [Machine Vision and Intelligence Group](https://www.mvig.org/) (supervised by [Prof. Cewu Lu](https://www.mvig.org/)) and [OpenRobotLab](https://github.com/OpenRobotLab) (supervised by [Dr. Jiangmiao Pang](https://oceanpang.github.io/)). 

My research interest lies in **Robot manipulation, Legged robots, Reinforcement learning, and Embodied AI**. During off time, I am a music enthusiast, fond of a wide range of genres including Hip-Hop, R&B, Rock, Pop, and Electronic. Sports take up much of my leisure as well, among which basketball is my favourite. I am also a fan of travelling and cinematography.
